gig listings

We have played at a few venues and being an unknown, all original band, we haven’t yet been able to score a weekend gig. This means we play mid week, and are out till way past our bed time. It also means that we don’t exactly pull huge crowds, even if we deserved to.

We do not gig heavily, but we do play enough to suit us. The benefit of playing in front of a live audience is that it makes you tighter and more practiced.

By the way, we are not alone; Perth is full of original bands, many of them really good, who mostly play mid week if at all, and whom no one has ever heard of. Some of them have played with us over time. We now have a good list of bands to play with who we know are reliable and will turn up and put on a good show. The only downside is that they are all like us and can only pull a tiny crowd. Ah well, that’s rock and roll.
