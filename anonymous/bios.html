Crew Bio

The Anonymous are a crew of underground misfits hailing from Perth W.A. Consisting of 2 members (Gribbz & Wisdom2th) with a distinctive style of original beats n' rhymes they've paved their own way through the local hip hop scene.

Forging an identity over the years through a devotion to their music & are now at the point of accomplishing their first album.

By producing & recording "The A. Team", they have molded a new sound within the midst of "aussie hip hop".

With the debut album & new live show under construction, they are a crew to look out for in the near future.

Keep it street, stay underground & don't pose ... PEACE OUT/
